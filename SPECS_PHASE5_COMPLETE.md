# Phase 5 Specifications Complete: Residents & Stories

**Date**: January 18, 2026
**Status**: ✅ Phase Complete

## Summary
Phase 5 breathes life into the simulation. It transforms residents from statistical units into individuals with names, jobs, needs, and dreams. It also adds the "Chaos" factor with crises.

---

## Specifications Created

### 1. RESIDENT_AMBITIONS.md ✅
**Purpose**: Gives residents long-term goals.
**Key Concepts**:
- Unique ambitions (Wealth, Housing, Career)
- "Ascension" rewards for fulfilling ambitions
- Failure leads to unhappiness/departure
- Adds narrative flavor to the swarm

### 2. EMPLOYMENT_SYSTEM.md ✅
**Purpose**: Simulates a real labor market.
**Key Concepts**:
- Residents physically commute to work
- Buildings need staff to operate
- Wages flow from buildings to residents
- Unemployment has consequences

### 3. SATISFACTION_MECHANICS.md ✅
**Purpose**: The primary feedback loop for player success.
**Key Concepts**:
- Aggregates needs (Food, Environment, Services) into a score
- Drives Immigration (Growth) and Emigration (Decline)
- Visual feedback (Icons/Bubbles)
- City Rating metric

### 4. CRISIS_MECHANICS.md ✅
**Purpose**: Emergent threats to test system resilience.
**Key Concepts**:
- Fire, Riot, Epidemic events
- Risk accumulation based on density/maintenance
- Active (Player) and Passive (Drone) response
- Spread mechanics (Fire jumps between rooms)

---

## How This Fits the Vision
Phase 5 delivers the "Soul" of the cyberpunk vision. It creates the stories—the tycoon who ascended, the riot that burned down the slums, the starvation crisis. It ensures the city feels alive, unpredictable, and morally complex.
